Mothers deserve a break.
Enjoy your break with us:

  • Meet new moms and build new friendships
  • Get involved in interesting, relaxing and useful activities
  • Enjoy your hobbies
  • Discover new interests
  • Develop new skills
  • Find your own reason to join us

Mothers Wellbeing Club is funded by Parents Association of St Aidans Primary School in Enniscorthy. It activates on a voluntary basis and undertakes a programme of activities that promotes the involvement of mothers.

Activities

Yoga Classes

Monday

11.00

Yoga can be a great way for a mother taking some time for your own health and wellbeing. It improves health, gives mental strength, balances metabolism, promotes flexibility, improves sleep, increases physical power, boosts confidence, tunes you in with your body.

Toddler Group

Tuesday

9.30

Toddler Group provides a relaxing environment for social interaction. It is a great opportunity to talk to other moms about challenges that arise, keeping your toddlers busy in different interesting activities. It also allows kids developing social skills at their own pace, building emotional confidence.

11.00

Arts and Crafts can be considered a form of diversional therapy, it helps you to self-express and manage your feelings, improves confidence and self-esteem, bolsters your mood and reduces stress overall. Think creatively and make something beautiful joining our Club.
