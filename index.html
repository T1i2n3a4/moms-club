Mothers deserve a break. Spend your break with us:

  • Meet new moms and build new friendships
  • Get involved in interesting, relaxing and useful activities
  • Enjoy your hobbies
  • Discover new interests
  • Develop new skills
  • Find your own reason to join us

Mothers Wellbeing Club is funded by Parents Association of St Aidans Primary School in Enniscorthy. It activates on a voluntary basis and undertakes a programme of activities that promotes the involvement of mothers.
